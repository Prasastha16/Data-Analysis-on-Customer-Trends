# Data-Analysis-on-Customer-Trends

### End-to-End Retail Customer Shopping Trends Analysis Using SQL, Python, and Power BI

## Overview

* Conducted an end-to-end retail customer shopping trends analysis.
* Utilized Python, SQL, and Power BI to transform raw data into actionable insights.
* Analyzed customer behavior, purchasing patterns, and sales performance.
* Developed interactive dashboards and business reports for decision-making.

## Dataset

* Retail customer shopping dataset containing customer demographics and transaction records.
* Key attributes include:

  * Customer ID
  * Age & Gender
  * Product Category
  * Purchase Amount
  * Payment Method
  * Location
  * Season
  * Purchase Frequency

## Tools & Technologies

* Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
* SQL (MySQL/PostgreSQL/SQL Server)
* Power BI
* Jupyter Notebook
* Gamma AI

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning

* Removed duplicate records.
* Handled missing values.
* Standardized column formats.
* Corrected inconsistent entries.

### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Analyzed customer demographics.
* Examined purchasing behavior and spending patterns.
* Identified top-performing product categories.
* Evaluated seasonal sales trends.
* Studied payment method preferences.

### 3. SQL Analysis

* Wrote business-focused SQL queries.
* Identified top-selling categories.
* Analyzed revenue by location.
* Segmented customers based on spending.
* Evaluated seasonal purchasing trends.

### 4. Power BI Dashboard

* Built an interactive business intelligence dashboard.
* Developed KPIs for sales and customer metrics.
* Visualized category performance and revenue trends.
* Added slicers and filters for dynamic analysis.

### 5. Reporting & Presentation

* Created a comprehensive analytics report.
* Designed a professional presentation using Gamma.
* Summarized findings and business recommendations.

## Key Insights

* Identified high-revenue product categories.
* Determined customer segments contributing the most sales.
* Discovered preferred payment methods across customers.
* Highlighted seasonal variations in shopping behavior.
* Revealed location-based purchasing trends.

## Results

* Converted raw retail data into actionable business insights.
* Improved understanding of customer purchasing behavior.
* Demonstrated proficiency in Python, SQL, and Power BI.
* Delivered an end-to-end analytics solution from data preparation to visualization.
